Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01.12.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Glow & Go Admin App collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when using the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Administrator data: email, display name, Firebase UID, role (owner/manager/staff), account status
  • Salon and business data: salon name, contact information, services offered, barber profiles, appointment details
  • Client data: client names, contact information (email/phone), appointment history, service preferences
  • Audit logs: records of administrative changes (who changed what, when), IP addresses for security purposes
  • Technical data: device type, OS version, app version, crash reports, and anonymous analytics for performance improvement

This data is collected when administrators sign in, manage services/barbers/appointments, create client records, or use app features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Managing and operating your salon (appointments, services, staff, clients)
  • Authenticating and authorizing administrators
  • Maintaining security and detecting abuse (audit logs, fraud prevention)
  • Improving app functionality and fixing bugs (crash reports, performance monitoring)
  • Communicating with administrators about updates or issues

We do not sell personal data to third parties or use it for third-party marketing.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure, including:

  • Firestore for structured data
  • Firebase Authentication for user credentials
  • Cloud Storage for files and backups
  • Firebase Crashlytics for error monitoring

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (SSL/TLS) and access controls limiting data to authorized personnel only.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, storage, and analytics
  • Email services: For sending invitations and notifications
  • Crashlytics: For monitoring app crashes and performance

These services have their own privacy policies and comply with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

5. Your Rights and Data Access

Administrators and salon owners have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Request correction or update of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@glowandgo.example.com

6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Administrators can request data deletion at any time, subject to legal hold requirements.

7. Super Admin Access

Super admin accounts have elevated access to manage multiple salons. All super admin actions are:

  • Recorded in audit logs with timestamps and details
  • Subject to role-based access controls
  • Monitored for unusual activity

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for use by children under 13 (or the legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data and notify the relevant parties.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced in the app and/or via email notification. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Serbia. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Lazarevac.
